Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

1 Introduction

Welcome to LearnLoom. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. When you visit our website including any other media form, media channel, mobile website, or mobile application related or connected thereto (collectively, the "Site"), you trust us with your personal information.

2 Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Site, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services (such as by filling out a form or booking a demo), or otherwise when you contact us.

The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the Site, the choices you make, and the products and features you use. The personal information we collect may include:

  • Names and Contact Data (Email, Phone Number)
  • Student details provided during registration

3 Use of Information

We use personal information collected via our Site for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process
  • To send you marketing and promotional communications (you can opt-out at any time)
  • To fulfill and manage your orders/bookings

4 Facebook Pixel and Tracking

We use Facebook Pixel and other tracking technologies to collect data about your behavior on our website. This data allows us to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and provide you with more relevant ads. The data collected is anonymous to us but is processed by Facebook and may be linked to your Facebook account.
