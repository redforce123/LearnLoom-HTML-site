About LearnLoom

We believe learning should be more than just textbooks and tests — it should spark curiosity, confidence, and creativity.

We are an online learning platform dedicated to helping students from Grades 1 to 10 build strong foundations in Math, Science, English, and Languages, while also preparing them for competitive exams like Math Olympiads, NAPLAN, and Selective Exams.

Our Story

LearnLoom was founded with one vision — to make learning interactive, meaningful, and future-ready for every child.

Anu Murali

Co-founder & Head of Academics

Anu began her journey as an Assistant Professor and Montessori guide. Holding Cambridge and AMI certifications, she blends traditional and modern methods to make every lesson engaging, creative, and concept-driven so that children don’t just learn—they love learning.

What Makes Us Different

Concept-first approach

Every lesson starts with clarity, ensuring children truly understand, not just memorize.

Interactive and fun

We weave in activities, games, simulations, and real-world examples so learning sticks.

Personalized guidance

Small batches, regular feedback, and progress reviews help every child grow at their own pace.

Global mindset

From Math problem-solving to language fluency, we prepare learners for opportunities beyond borders.

Our name, LearnLoom, reflects our mission — weaving together knowledge, skills, and creativity into a strong fabric that supports lifelong learning.

At LearnLoom, we’re not just teaching subjects — we’re building future-ready learners who are confident, curious, and capable of achieving their dreams.
