The Enrollment Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

Follow our simple four-step process to get your child started on their learning journey with us.

1

Step 1: Book a Demo

Navigate to the 'Book Demo' section and fill in the form. This helps us tailor the live demonstration to your child's needs.

2

Step 2: Attend the Demo

Join the scheduled session to experience our interactive teaching style and see how our curriculum works firsthand.

3

Step 3: Meet a Coordinator

After the demo, a coordinator will recommend classes and discuss payment plans based on the student's performance.

4

Step 4: Finalize Enrollment

Once you're satisfied with the plan, we'll complete the enrollment, and the student can officially begin their classes.

The LearnLoom Method: Our Three-C Approach

At LearnLoom, we use the "Three Cs" to guide our teaching and help students succeed.

Concept Clarity

We visualize and connect new ideas to a child's everyday life, ensuring they understand concepts easily and deeply.

Confidence First

We provide consistent encouragement and celebrate every success, big or small, to build a strong sense of self-belief in every student.

Creative Thinking

We encourage kids to think outside the box and solve problems in new ways. Our goal isn't just to teach them, but to inspire them to think creatively.
